Hybrid Markov Chain & Neural ODE for Irregular Time-Series Prediction

Ongoing research project supervised by Prof Fan Xiuyi, developing a hybrid framework that combines Markov models with Neural Ordinary Differential Equations (Neural ODEs) to model and predict irregularly sampled patient vital-sign time series.

The goal is to identify algorithms that are both interpretable and accurate for forecasting clinical signals such as heart rate, blood pressure, and oxygen saturation under non-uniform sampling conditions.
